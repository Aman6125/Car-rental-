⚙️ Process

Ride in 4 simple steps

From browsing to boarding — the whole journey takes under 2 minutes.

01
Step One
Enter your pickup & drop

Type in your starting point and destination. Our smart location search auto-suggests addresses across India in real-time.

02
Step Two
Choose your car type

Select from Mini, Sedan, SUV, or Luxury. Browse our full fleet with transparent per-km pricing, ratings, and availability status.

03
Step Three
Confirm & pay securely

Review your estimated fare, select payment method (UPI, card, or cash), and confirm your booking. You'll receive an instant confirmation with booking ID.

04
Step Four
Enjoy your ride!

Your verified driver arrives at the scheduled time. Track your ride live, rate your experience, and earn loyalty points for your next journey.
