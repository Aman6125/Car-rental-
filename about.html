ℹ️ Our Story

We're reimagining
how India moves

SwiftRide was founded in 2022 with a simple belief: every person deserves a safe, comfortable, and affordable ride — whether they're a student commuting to college or a CEO flying to a meeting.

We've grown from a small Bengaluru startup to a pan-India platform serving 50,000+ happy riders across 200+ cities. And we're just getting started.

🎯 Mission

Moving people forward, one ride at a time

Our mission is to make premium mobility accessible to everyone — not just the privileged few. We believe technology can democratize transportation while preserving dignity and safety.

Every driver partner on our platform is a valued collaborator. We provide them with fair earnings, flexible hours, and growth opportunities — because a thriving driver community means a better experience for riders.

💡 Values

What we stand for
